Dear Mr. Chairman and Mr. Speaker:
This is to inform you that I have signed into law House Local Bill No. 24-6, entitled "To appropriate
the remaining fund balance of $50,411.00 under Saipan Local law 21-10 (Project Code 7192H) for
the maintenance, repairs, and improvements of Commonwealth parks and recreational facilities in
Precinct IV and for the purchase of base coarse to repair secondary roadways in Precinct IV; and for
other purposes," which was passed by the Saipan and Northern Islands Legislative Delegation of the
Twenty-Fourth Northern Marianas Commonwealth Legislature.
This Bill becomes Saipan Local Law 24-01. Copies bearing my signature are forwarded for your
reference.
Sincerely,

A LOCAL APPROPRIATION BILL FOR AN ACT
FOR THE THIRD SENATORIAL DISTRICT
To appropriate the remaining fund balance of $50,411.00 under Saipan Local Law
21-10 (Project Code 7192H) for the maintenance, repairs and improvements of
Commonwealth parks and recreational facilities in Precinct IV and for the purchase
of base coarse to repair secondary roadways in Precinct IV; and for other purposes.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE THIRD SENATORIAL DISTRICT DELEGATION PURSUANT TO
CHAPTER 4, DIVISION 1, TITLE 1 OF THE COMMONWEALTH CODE:
1 SECTION 1. PURPOSE.
2 The purpose of this Act is to appropriate the remaining fund balance of $50,411.00 under
3 Saipan Local Law 21-10 (Project Code 7192H) for the maintenance, repairs and improvements of
4 Commonwealth parks and recreational facilities in Precinct IV and for the purchase of base coarse
5 and the rental of necessary equipment to facilitate the repair of secondary roadways in Precinct IV
6 and for other purposes.
7 SECTION 2. RE-APPROPRIATION.
8 Notwithstanding any provision(s) of law, statutes, rules or regulations to the contrary and
9 pursuant to funds allocated to the Third Senatorial District the Saipan and Northern Islands
Saipan Local Law No. 24-01
HOUSE LOCAL BILL No. 24-6
1 Legislative Delegation (SNILD) hereby appropriates the remaining fund balance of $50,411.00
2 under Saipan Local Law 21-10 (Project Code 7192H); as follows:
3 (a) Thirty-Five Thousand Four Hundred Eleven dollars ($35,411.00) to the Division of
4 Parks and Recreation for the maintenance, repairs and improvements of
5 Commonwealth parks and recreational facilities in Precinct IV; and
6 (b) Fifteen Thousand ($15,000.00) to the Division of Roads and Grounds for the purchase
7 of base coarse and the rental of necessary equipment to facilitate the repair of secondary
8 roadways in Precinct IV.
9 SECTION 3. EXPENDITURE AUTHORITY
10 The expenditure authority for the funds under Section 2(a) shall be the Director of Parks
11 and Recreation in consultation with the Secretary of the Department of Lands and Natural
12 Resources and the expenditure authority for the funds under Section 2(b) shall be the Director of
13 Roads and Grounds in consultation with the Secretary of the Department of Public Works.
14 SECTION 4. REPROGRAMMING.
15 Notwithstanding any laws, provisions, or regulations to the contrary, funds appropriated
16 under this act shall not be reprogrammed for any other purpose.
17 SECTION 5. FISCAL YEAR.
18 The funds allocated under this Act shall be without fiscal year limitation(s).
19 SECTION 6. REPORTING OF EXPENDITURES.
20 Expenditures of the funds allocated under Section 2 of this Act shall be responsible to
21 report all expenses to the Chairperson of the Saipan and Northern Islands Legislative Delegation
2
Saipan Local Law No. 24-01
HOUSE LOCAL BILL No. 24-6
1 a detail report showing the expenditure of the funds. Provided further that failure to provide all
2 expenses, entities that were afforded funds under this Act shall not be able to avail to any future
3 funds appropriated by the Saipan and Northern Islands Legislative Delegation.
4 SECTION 7. FUND DISTRIBUTION.
5 Funds appropriated under Section 2 of this Act shall be distributed accordingly to the
6 intended purpose and or entity upon this Act becoming law.
7 SECTION 8. SEVERABILITY.
8 If any provision of this Act or the application of any such provision to any person or
9 circumstance should be held invalid by a court of competent jurisdiction, the remainder of this Act
10 or the application of its provisions to persons or circumstances other than those to which it is held
11 invalid shall not be affected thereby.
12 SECTION 9. SAVINGS CLAUSE.
13 This Act and any repealer contained herein shall not be construed as affecting any existing
14 right acquired under contract or acquired under statutes repealed or under any rule, regulation or
15 order adopted under the statutes. Repealers contained in this Act shall not affect any proceeding
16 instituted under or pursuant to prior law. The enactment of the Act shall not have the effect of
17 terminating, or in any way modifying, any liability, civil or criminal, which shall already be in
18 existence on the date this Act becomes effective.
19 SECTION 10. EFFECTIVE DATE.
20 This Act shall take effect upon its approval by the Governor or it becoming law without
21 such approval.
