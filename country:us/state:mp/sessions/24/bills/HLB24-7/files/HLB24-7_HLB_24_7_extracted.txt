RE: Collection of Poker Fees on Tinian
Dear Mayor Aldan,
This is to inform you that on March 28, 2025, K-Amusement made a payment to the Department
of Finance on Tinian in the amount of $168,000 for the renewal of fourteen (14) poker machine
licenses. All payments have been deposited into the CNMI Treasury account on March 28, 2025.
In accordance with Tinian Local Law 23-10, the annual local license fee for Tinian would be
$84,000.
Should you require additional information in regards to this matter, please do not hesitate to contact
me via email ma.mendiolaedof.aov.mp or (670) 433-1600.
Sincerely,
Milissa A. Mendiola
Resident Department Head
Department of Finance
