Dear Mr. Chairman and Mr. Speaker:
This is to inform you that I have signed into law House Local Bill No. 24-1, D1, entitled "To
appropriate $11,877.00 for the personnel and operations of the Tinian Casino Gaming Control
Commission pursuant to 10 CMC § 2581 and 1 CMC § 1405.", which was passed by the Tinian and
Aguiguan Legislative Delegation of the Twenty-Fourth Northern Marianas Commonwealth
Legislature.
This Bill becomes Tinian Local Law 24-01. Copies bearing my signature are forwarded for your
reference.

SECOND SENATORIAL DISTRICT
TWENTY-FOURTH NORTHERN MARIANAS COMMONWEALTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION, 2025 H. L. B. 24-1, D1
A LOCAL APPROPRIATION BILL FOR AN ACT
FOR THE SECOND SENATORIAL DISTRICT
To appropriate $11,877.00 for the personnel and operations of the Tinian Casino
Gaming Control Commission pursuant to 10 CMC § 2581 and 1 CMC § 1405.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE SECOND SENATORIAL DISTRICT DELEGATION PURSUANT
TO CHAPTER 4, DIVISION 1, TITLE 1 OF THE COMMONWEALTH CODE:
1 SECTION 1. PURPOSE.
2 The purpose of this Act is to appropriate Eleven Thousand Eight Hundred Seventy-Seven
3 dollars ($11,877.00 USD) generated from local revenue pursuant to 10 CMC § 2581 for personnel
4 and operations under the Tinian Casino Gaming Control Commission. The certification of funds
5 was prepared on January 21, 2025, and was reviewed and confirmed by the Tinian Municipal
6 Treasurer
7 SECTION 2. APPROPRIATIONS.
8 Notwithstanding any provision(s) of law, statutes, rules or regulations to the contrary and
9 pursuant to funds allocated to the Second Senatorial District from local revenue, the Tinian and
Tinian Local Law No. 24-01
HOUSE LOCAL BILL No. 24-1, D1
1 Aguiguan Legislative Delegation hereby appropriates $11,877.00 of the revenues collected for the
2 Second Senatorial District as follows:
3 (A) $11,877.00 to the Tinian Casino Gaming Control Commission for personnel and
4 operations.
5 SECTION 3. EXPENDITURE AUTHORITY.
6 The expenditure authority for funds under Section 2 shall be the Chairman of the Tinian
7 Casino Gaming Control Commission.
8 SECTION 4. REPROGRAMMING.
9 Funds appropriated under this act shall not be reprogrammed for any other purpose and
10 notwithstanding any provision of law, all funds appropriated under this Act shall be without fiscal
11 year limitation(s).
12 SECTION 5. SEVERABILITY.
13 If any provision of this Act or the application of any such provision to any person or
14 circumstance should be held invalid by a court of competent jurisdiction, the remainder of this Act
15 or the application of its provisions to persons or circumstances other than those to which it is held
16 invalid shall not be affected thereby.
17 SECTION 6. SAVINGS CLAUSE.
18 This Act and any repealer contained herein shall not be construed as affecting any existing
19 right acquired under contract or acquired under statutes repealed or under any rule, regulation or
20 order adopted under the statutes. Repealers contained in this Act shall not affect any proceeding
21 instituted under or pursuant to prior law. The enactment of the Act shall not have the effect of
2

HOUSE LOCAL BILL No. 24-1, D1
terminating, or in any way modifying, any liability, civil or criminal, which shall already be in
existence on the date this Act becomes effective.

Section 2:
SECTION 7. EFFECTIVE DATE.
This Act shall take effect upon its approval by the Governor or it becoming law without
such approval.
