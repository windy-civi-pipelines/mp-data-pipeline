Section 1:
TWENTY-FOURTH NORTHERN MARIANAS COMMONWEALTH
LEGISLATURE
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
/9
Regular Session, 2025 H. B. 24-
To rename the "CNMI Scholarship Office" to the "CNMI
Scholarship and Financial Assistance Office."
BE IT ENACTED BY THE 24TH NORTHERN MARIANAS
COMMONWEALTH LEGISLATURE:
1 Section 1. Findings and Purpose. The Legislature finds that increasing
2 access to education and workforce training through financial aid opportunities
3 significantly improves the likelihood that local residents will become well-
4 educated, well-equipped contributors to the CNMI's workforce. Currently, the
5 CNMI Scholarship Office provides such financial aid opportunities through
6 multiple scholarships, such as the Post Secondary Teacher Education Program
7 Scholarship and the Behavioral Health Professional Scholarship.
8 On July 11, 2024, the CNMI Scholarship Board of Directors approved
9 changing the name of the "CNMI Scholarship Office" to the "CNMI Scholarship
10 and Financial Assistance Office" to better reflect the scope of the office's services.
11 These services include, but are not limited to, administering higher education
12 financial assistance programs, which necessarily includes the power to address
2 / 9
HOUSE BILL
1 matters related to delinquent or defaulted financial assistance agreements, and other
2 responsibilities necessary to carry out the office's mission.
3 Based on the foregoing, the Legislature concurs with the CNMI Scholarship
4 Board of Directors' decision to rename the office as it more accurately represents
5 the office's purpose and function. Therefore, the purpose of this Act is to rename
6 the "CNMI Scholarship Office" to the "CNMI Scholarship and Financial
7 Assistance Office."
8 Section 2. Amendment. Section 10102 of Chapter 10, Division 1, Title 3
9 of the Commonwealth Code is hereby amended to read as follows:
10 "ยง 10102. CNMI Scholarship and Financial Assistance Office
11 (a) The CNMI Scholarship and Financial Assistance Office is hereby
12 established within the Executive Branch headed by an administrator appointed by
13 the Scholarship Board of Directors.
14 (b) The CNMI Scholarship and Financial Assistance Office is established
15 as an autonomous agency under the general control and direction of the Scholarship
16 Board of Directors. The board shall adopt bylaws that enumerate its purposes,
17 duties, powers, and rules of organization and operation, which bylaws shall not be
18 inconsistent with this article."
19 Section 3. Global Amendment. Unless the context clearly indicates
20 otherwise, all references in the Commonwealth Code, regulations, executive orders,

10 provisions to persons or circumstances other than those to which it is held invalid

19 Section 6. Effective Date. This Act shall take effect upon its approval by
20 the Governor, or it becoming law without such approval.
