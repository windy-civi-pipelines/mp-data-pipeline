3 "program countries"; and
4 WHEREAS, the criteria for a "program country" designation include
5 passport security, a nonimmigrant visa refusal rate below three percent (3%), as
6 well as reciprocal visa waivers for U.S. passport holders; and
7 WHEREAS, as of 2025, the Philippine passport ranks as the world's 75th
th
8 most powerful, bumping up from 77 in 2021, with access to 65 nations without a
9 visa; and
10 WHEREAS, each Philippine ePassport contains a Radio Frequency
11 Identification (RFID) microchip to securely store information and help protect
12 against safety threats and identity theft, an essential requirement for program
13 countries; and
14 WHEREAS, the Philippines' nonimmigrant B-visa refusal rate in the U.S.
15 is 28.33% for Fiscal Year 2024; and
16 WHEREAS, according to the October 2024 U.S. Congressional Research
17 Service Visa Waiver Program report, "Under statute, the Secretary of Homeland
18 Security has the authority to waive the nonimmigrant visa refusal rate requirement";

5 visa; and
6 WHEREAS, U.S. passport holders have long been allowed to enter the
7 Philippines without a visa; and
8 WHEREAS, the Philippines and the United States maintain a close security
9 alliance due to the 1951 U.S.-Philippines Mutual Defense Treaty, 1998 Visiting
10 Forces Agreement, and more recently, the 2014 Enhanced Defense Cooperation
11 Agreement (EDCA) which authorizes U.S. forces' access to designated bases in the
12 Philippines for strategic joint military operations and training, humanitarian
13 assistance, and disaster relief activities; and
14 WHEREAS, granting the Philippines visa-free entry to the CNMI will
15 strengthen the U.S.-Philippines partnership, reinforcing joint military defense
16 efforts and furthering shared goals of security and democracy;
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the House of
18 Representatives of the Twenty-Fourth Northern Marianas Commonwealth
19 Legislature, the Senate concurring, urgently requests for the immediate inclusion
20 of the Republic of the Philippines in the nonimmigrant Guam-CNMI Visa Waiver
21 Program administered by the United States Department of Homeland Security; and
