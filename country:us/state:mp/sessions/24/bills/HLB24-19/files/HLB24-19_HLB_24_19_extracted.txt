TWENTY-FOURTH NORTHERN MARIANAS COMMONWEALTH
LEGISLATURE
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
q
SESSION, 2025 H. L. B. 24- /
FOR THE THIRD SENATORIAL DISTRICT
To appropriate the remaining fund balance of $260,670.00 from the
Third Senatorial District Developers Infrastructure Tax Fund allotted to
Precinct I under Saipan Local Law 22-16; and for other purposes.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE THIRD SENATORIAL DISTRICT
DELEGATION PURSUANT TO CHAPTER 4, DIVISION 1, TITLE 1 OF
THE COMMONWEALTH CODE:
1 SECTION 1. PURPOSE.
2 The purpose of this Act is to appropriate the remaining fund balance of
3 $260,670.00 from the Third Senatorial District Developers Infrastructure Tax Fund
4 allotted to Precinct I under Saipan Local Law 22-16. Attached for reference is the
5 Year-To-Date Budget Report in the amount of $260,670.00, dated July 3, 2025.
6 SECTION 2. RE-APPROPRIATION.
7 Notwithstanding any provision(s) of law, statutes, rules or regulations to the
8 contrary and pursuant to funds allocated to the Third Senatorial District the Saipan
9 and Northern Islands Legislative Delegation (SNILD) hereby appropriates the
HOUSE LOCAL BILL 24- pf
1 remaining fund balance of $260,670.00 under Saipan Local Law 22-16, Section
2 2(a); as follows:
3 (A) $260,670.00 for the pavement of Halle Place Road in Precinct I. The
4 expenditure authority shall be the Secretary of the Department of Public
5 Works or his/her designee.
6 SECTION 3. REPROGRAMMING.
7 Notwithstanding any laws, provisions, or regulations to the contrary, funds
8 appropriated under this act shall not be reprogrammed for any other purpose.
9 SECTION 4. FISCAL YEAR.
10 The funds allocated under this Act shall be without fiscal year limitation(s).
11 SECTION 5. REPORTING OF EXPENDITURES.
12 Expenditures of the funds allocated under Section 2 of this Act shall be
13 responsible to report all expenses to the Chairperson of the Saipan and Northern
14 Islands Legislative Delegation a detail report showing the expenditure of the funds.
15 Provided further that failure to provide all expenses, entities that were afforded
16 funds under this Act shall not be able to avail to any future funds appropriated by
17 the Saipan and Northern Islands Legislative Delegation.
18 SECTION 6. FUND DISTRIBUTION.
19 Funds appropriated under Section 2 of this Act shall be distributed
20 accordingly to the intended purpose and or entity upon this Act becoming law.
21 SECTION 7. SEVERABILITY.
2
/4
HOUSE LOCAL BILL 24-
1 If any provision of this Act or the application of any such provision to any
2 person or circumstance should be held invalid by a court of competent jurisdiction,
3 the remainder of this Act or the application of its provisions to persons or
4 circumstances other than those to which it is held invalid shall not be affected
5 thereby.
6 SECTION 8. SAVINGS CLAUSE.
7 This Act and any repealer contained herein shall not be construed as
8 affecting any existing right acquired under contract or acquired under statutes
9 repealed or under any rule, regulation or order adopted under the statutes. Repealers
10 contained in this Act shall not affect any proceeding instituted under or pursuant to
11 prior law. The enactment of the Act shall not have the effect of terminating, or in
12 any way modifying, any liability, civil or criminal, which shall already be in
13 existence on the date this Act becomes effective.
14 SECTION 9. EFFECTIVE DATE.
15 This Act shall take effect upon its approval by the Governor or it becoming
16 law without such approval.
