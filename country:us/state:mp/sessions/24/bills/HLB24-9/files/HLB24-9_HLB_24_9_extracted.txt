TWENTY-FOURTH NORTHERN MARIANAS COMMONWEALTH
LEGISLATURE
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
SESSION, 2025 H. L. B.
FOR THE FIRST SENATORIAL DISTRICT
To amend Rota Local Law 23-15, to appropriate One Hundred Fifty-
Five Thousand Dollars ($155,000.00) for the continued operation of
the Rota Casino Gaming Commission; and for other purposes.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE FIRST SENATORIAL DISTRICT
DELEGATION PURSUANT TO CHAPTER 4, DIVISION 1, TITLE 1 OF
THE COMMONWEALTH CODE:
1 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Section 1 of Rota Local Law 23-15 is amended to
2 read:
3 "Section 1. Purpose. The purpose of this bill is to appropriate amend Rota
4 Local Law 23-15 l ocal funds in the amount of, which appropriated One Hundred
5 Fifty-Five Thousand Dollars ($155,000.00) for the continued operation of the Rota
6 Casino Gaming Commission. These funds were received by the Municipality of
7 Rota pursuant to 10 CMC § 12141. This local appropriation is made in accordance
8 with NMI CONST. art. VI § 8, cl. 2; 1 CMC §§ 1402-03, and 1408; and 10 CMC §
9 12103. This appropriation is based on the budget request adopted and approved by
HOUSE LOCAL BILL 2 10 0'47
1 the 19th Rota Municipal Council during its 13th Regular Session, held on August
2 5, 2024.
3 The Rota Legislative Delegation finds it necessary to provide funds for the
4 RCGC."
5 SECTION 2. AMENDMENT. Rota Local Law 23-15, sections 2, 3, 4, and 5, are
6 amended to read:
7 "Section 2. APPROPRIATION.
8 Notwithstanding any provisions, statutes, laws or regulations to the
9 contrary, the Rota Legislative Delegation hereby appropriates, from the funds
10 received by the Municipality of Rota pursuant to 10 CMC § 12141, One Hundred
11 Fifty-Five Thousand Dollars ($155,000.00) is appropriated_as follows:
12 (a) $105,000 for personnel salaries including fringe benefits;
13 (b) $15,000 for RCGC operations, equipment, and supplies;
14 (c) $15,000 for training expenses;
15 (d) $10,000 for legal counsel; and
16 (e) $10,000 initial deposit in a special account for payments owed,
17 including fringe benefits, to current and past RGCC members. The
18 Rota Municipal Treasurer or Mayor's designee shall establish a special
19 account, the RCGC Members Payment Special Account. The special
20 account shall remain open for future appropriations until the past-due
21 payments are paid in full. Provided however the funds in the special

4 (1) Name of the RCGC Member;

7 (4) Balance owed to the Commission Member.
8 Provided further that the required written certification shall be
9 provided to the Mayor of Rota, the Rota Municipal Council Chairperson,

13 be the RCGC Chairperson.

1 The expenditure authority shall submit detailed quarterly reports showing
2 the expenditure of funds appropriated under this Act. These reports shall be
3 furnished to the Chairpersons of the Rota Municipal Council and the First
4 Senatorial Delegation until all appropriated funds have been fully expended. If the

6 expenditures shall be authorized under this Act until the required reports are
7 submitted."
8 SECTION 3. SEVERABILITY.
9 If any provision of this Act or the application of any such provision to any
10 person or circumstance should be held invalid by a court of competent jurisdiction,
11 the remainder of this Act or the application of its provisions to persons or
12 circumstances other than those to which it is held invalid shall not be affected
13 thereby.
14 SECTION 4. SAVINGS CLAUSE.
15 This Act and any repealer contained herein shall not be construed as
16 affecting any existing right acquired under contract or acquired under statutes
17 repealed or under any rule, regulation or order adopted under the statutes. Repealers
18 contained in this Act shall not affect any proceeding instituted under or pursuant to
19 prior law. The enactment of the Act shall not have the effect of terminating, or in
20 any way modifying, any liability, civil or criminal, which shall already be in
21 existence on the date this Act becomes effective.
